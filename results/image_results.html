This repository is a multiple linear regression of oceanic climate indices (plus atmospheric QBO) applying annual modulated tidal forcing. Select SLH models as well in this set, such as Brest, Honolulu, Ft. Denison, which is then comprehensively treated featuring many other sites. This model is adapted from Mathematical GeoEnergy (Wiley/AGU, 2018).

This page is dedicate to providing cross-validated (CV) results across the time-series indices. The cross-validation essentially trains on a given interval of a time-series, and then an excluded area is used as test interval. So an identifier such as 0.4-0.5 indicates that the fractional span 0.4-0.5 of the entire time-series is used as a test/validation interval and the enclosing region is used for training. When this was initially created on 9/1/2025, there were 3000 CV results on time-series that averaged around 100 years, or around 1200 monthly readings apiece.
